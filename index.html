Hello World, I'm Magy. Nice to meet you!

I want to start off by mentioning that I find joy in wearing many hats. Before starting my freelancing career 5 years ago, I had the wonderful opportunity to help teach tiny humans. As a former early childhood teacher, I know what it’s like to perform a daily balancing act in order to pivot between projects constantly throughout the day. Throughout my early career, I added project management, effective team collaboration, and creative problem-solving to the top of my skill sets.

I later decided to delve into the field of digital marketing in which I quickly discovered the significance of data analytics and what it meant for businesses. Soon after I joined an e-commerce brand as a Digital Experience Specialist. This opportunity led me to collaborate with engineering and creative teams on product design and web development.

Using my recently acquired skillset and love for web development, I began to offer website redesigns to various small businesses and nonprofit organizations. A stronger online presence means having a greater impact overall. I knew I could help organizations and businesses stay relevant in the digital age by maintaining and improving their visibility.

The next part of my journey leads me to seek new projects centered around positive social benefits. I’m on a mission to support the development of new technologies offering creative solutions to current (and future) social problems. I look forward to collaborating with you on your next project. Thanks for reading!
